Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 01 January 2026

1. Introduction

Vasool Management respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Name, phone number, email address
  • Account credentials
  • Customer, loan, and payment data entered by you
  • Business and financial records you manage in the App

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (OS, device type)
  • App usage statistics
  • Log and diagnostic data

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain the App
  • Process transactions and records
  • Improve features and performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

4. Biometric Authentication

The App may support biometric login (fingerprint or facial recognition).

  • Biometric data is handled entirely by your device's operating system
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit biometric data

5. Data Storage and Security

We apply industry-standard security measures, including encryption and access controls. However, no digital system can guarantee 100% security.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data.

Data may be shared only:

  • With your consent
  • With trusted service providers under confidentiality agreements
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect legal rights and safety
  • During mergers or business transfers (with notice)

7. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party services for:

  • Cloud hosting
  • Authentication
  • Notifications
  • Analytics and performance monitoring

These providers receive only the minimum data required to perform their services.

8. Partner & Shared Access Features

If you use partner features, you control what data is shared. You are responsible for granting access only to trusted parties.

9. Data Ownership

You retain full ownership of all data entered into the App. We do not claim ownership of your business, customer, or financial records.

10. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services or meet legal obligations. Upon account deletion, data will be deleted or anonymized unless legally required otherwise.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country. Appropriate safeguards are applied to protect your information.

12. Communications

We may send service-related notifications via in-app messages or push notifications. You can manage preferences where applicable.

13. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect children's data.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date.

15. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us through the App's support section.
